About Our Church

Building a Community of Faith

Our Mission

To be a welcoming community where people of all backgrounds can experience the love of Jesus Christ, grow in faith, and serve others with compassion and dedication.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant fellowship where English and Romanian speakers worship together, celebrating our cultural heritage while building bridges across our community.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the power of God's Word, the importance of worship, the value of fellowship, and a commitment to living out the Gospel through service, love, and honest relationships.

Our Story

Fellowship International Romanian Church was founded to serve the Romanian community in Southwest Florida while welcoming people of all backgrounds. We are committed to maintaining our cultural traditions while embracing the diversity of our community. Through bilingual services, we create a bridge between generations and cultures, all united in Christ.
