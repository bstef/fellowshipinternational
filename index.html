Despre Noi · About Us

Who We Are

Fellowship International Romanian Church is a Spirit-filled, Christ-centered community located in the heart of Rotonda West, Florida. We are a bilingual congregation — worshipping in both English and Romanian — united by our love for Jesus and our love for people.

We believe the Church is a family, and every person who walks through our doors is welcomed as part of that family. Whether you are Romanian, English-speaking, or simply seeking God, there is a place for you here.

Faith in Christ

Jesus is the foundation of everything we do, believe, and celebrate together.

Bilingual Community

Rooted in Romanian heritage, open to all — worship in both English and Romanian.

Genuine Fellowship

Real relationships built around the Word, the table, and shared life together.

Welcoming to All

Every background, every story — you belong here just as you are.

Our Heart & Mission

We are more than a church — we are a family gathering in the name of Jesus. Our mission is simple: to love God deeply, love people unconditionally, and share the hope of the Gospel with everyone around us.

Every Sunday we come together to worship, share a meal, and grow in faith — in English, in Romanian, and in the universal language of God's love.
