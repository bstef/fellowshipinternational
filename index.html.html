A Place to Belong,
A Family to Grow With

Whether you're joining us for the first time or returning home, Fellowship International Romanian Church is a warm, Spirit-filled community rooted in faith, family, and the love of Christ. We welcome everyone — Romanian, English-speaking, and all who seek the living God.

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." — Matthew 18:20
